Testing & Commissioning of Substation Equipment

We provide end-to-end testing and commissioning services for high-voltage and medium-voltage substations. Our scope covers the full range of substation components critical for the stability and safety of your power system:

Power Transformer Testing
→ Assessment of insulation resistance, ratio test, vector group, winding resistance, oil quality, and more.

Circuit Breaker Testing
→ Verification of tripping time, contact resistance, insulation, timing characteristics, and operating mechanisms.

Current & Voltage Transformer Testing
→ Polarity checks, ratio and burden testing, insulation resistance, and secondary injection.

Isolator & Earth Switch Testing
→ Mechanical operation, continuity test, contact pressure testing, and earth switch functionality.

Surge Arrester Testing
→ Leakage current measurement, insulation test, and monitoring of protection behavior against overvoltages.

These services ensure your substation equipment operates with precision and meets national and international standards.

Configuration, Testing & Commissioning of Numerical Relays

Numerical relays are the brain of modern protection systems. Our team offers complete solutions for testing, configuring, and commissioning a wide range of relays from leading manufacturers

Micom Relay Testing
→ Configuration, logic setting, communication check (IEC 61850), and scheme validation.

ABB Make Relay Testing
→ Testing of REF, RET, and other ABB relays including system integration and event logging.

Siemens Make Relay Testing
→ Testing and parameter setting of SIPROTEC series, scheme testing, and trip circuit verification.

Other Make Relays
→ Support for GE, Schneider, SEL, and other industry-standard protective relays.

Thermal Imaging & Infrared Inspections

Detecting potential faults before they cause failures.

Transformer & Switchgear Testing

Maintaining critical power infrastructure.

Preventive Maintenance Testing

Minimizing downtime and extending equipment lifespan.
